About the Visibility Score:

The visibility score calculates the amount of space that is directly visible in the 15 feet around the front and front-passenger side of the vehicle, from ground level to six feet above ground. The visibility score is the percent of volume that is directly visible to the driver. Note that the visibility score will vary across drivers with different heights or drivers positioned differently in their seats.

Although the visibility score was originally a single number, the VIEW app team has recently divided it into front and passenger-side visibility scores to allow for more precision and understanding of vehicle blind zones.
